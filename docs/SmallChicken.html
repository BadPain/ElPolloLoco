Class: SmallChicken

SmallChicken()

new SmallChicken()

Constructor for SmallChicken objects. Initializes the SmallChicken with a random x position between 1750 and 3500, and a random speed between 0.15 and 0.65. Loads the walking animations for the chicken. Sets the initial state of the chicken to be not dead. Calls the animate function to start animating the chicken.

Methods

animate()

Initiates the animation intervals for the small chicken. The small chicken moves left and plays walking animations at specified intervals, unless the small chicken is dead or has moved beyond the left boundary.
